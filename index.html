TEAMTRACKER

Hockey team management traditionally requires a lot of manual interaction with players and rosters to provide the best experience for all players on game day. This experience for both the players and the managers relies on manual seasonal and weekly communications to ensure successful attendance. A well-managed hockey team would benefit from a digital presence to communicate this critical season and gameday information to all current players and prospective players. A web based tool would allow the team manager to reach the entire player audience with relevant team information 24/7 and cut down on email communication with simple information requests.
